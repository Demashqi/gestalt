We offer customised training and workshops to provide an in-depth knowledge and equip the workforce with the necessary coping skills to deal with mental health issues, such as building grit, emotional intelligence, dealing with substance abuse and the mental health benefits of a good nutrition. 

  • Build resilience
  • Optimise well-being
  • Emotions management
  • Nutrition
  • Managing substance use and abuse
  • Moving from languishing to flourishing
  • Training to recognise psychosocial hazards & how to manage risks in the workplace on a management as well as individual/employee level
  • Training to workers and employers to identify signs of exposure to psychosocial risks

Workshops include stress
reduction techniques such as:

  • Yoga
