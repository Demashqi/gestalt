About Us

WHO WE ARE

Gestalt Training & Consultancy was founded in 2021 to provide a 360° solution to the increasing mental health issues arising in workplaces across the world, including Mauritius, which were amplified by the pandemic.

OUR VISION

Our vision is to transform workplaces and the workforce in Mauritius into mentally healthy and flourishing ones.

SOCIAL ENTERPRISE

Our envisioned social enterprise uses part of the profits made from companies to offer our services for free or at a reduced fee to NGOs and non-profit organisations working towards creating social value and fighting social ills.

Moreover, our efforts to create a healthy society cannot be to the detriment of the planet. We have taken the initiative to reduce our waste, collaborate with eco-conscious partners and track and reduce our carbon footprint.
